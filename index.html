Getting started

What the first few weeks look like

01

A free 20-minute consultation

A short call to talk about what's bringing you in, ask any questions, and get a feel for whether this is a good fit — no pressure either way.

02

An intake session

A longer first appointment to get the full picture: history, current stressors, and what you're hoping is different six months from now.

03

Weekly sessions

Most clients meet weekly at first, then move to biweekly as things stabilize. Sessions are 50 minutes, held via secure video through SimplePractice's telehealth platform — you'll get a private link for each appointment.

Practical details

Questions before you reach out

Do you accept insurance?

Yes — I'm in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield. For other insurers, I'm out-of-network but provide a superbill that many clients submit for partial reimbursement. I'm happy to walk through what that looks like on our consultation call.

What does a session cost?

For Blue Cross Blue Shield clients, cost depends on your plan's copay or coinsurance. Self-pay sessions are $175 for individuals and $195 for couples, for 50 minutes. A limited number of sliding-scale spots are available — ask during your consultation if cost is a barrier.

Is telehealth actually effective?

Yes — research consistently supports its effectiveness, and some studies even find better follow-through with telehealth than other formats. All sessions are held over secure video, which also means we're not limited by your location within the state.

How do I know if I need therapy or just a vacation?

Often it's not either/or. The consultation call is a low-stakes way to find out — there's no obligation to continue afterward.
